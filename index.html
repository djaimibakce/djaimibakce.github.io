Professional Profile

Dr. Djaimi Bakce is an Associate Professor in Agricultural Economics at Universitas Riau, Indonesia. He serves as Coordinator of the Center for Plantation, Peatland and Rural Development Studies. His research focuses on agribusiness development, production efficiency, rural economy, and sustainable agricultural systems, particularly in wetland ecosystems. He has extensive experience in research, policy advisory, and community development, collaborating with government institutions, international organizations, and industry partners.
