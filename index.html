Professional Profile

Dr. Djaimi Bakce is an Associate Professor in Agricultural Economics at Universitas Riau, Indonesia. His research focuses on agribusiness systems, production efficiency, rural development, and environmental sustainability, particularly in wetland and peatland ecosystems. He has led more than 80 research projects and actively contributes as an international journal reviewer and consultant for government and international agencies.
